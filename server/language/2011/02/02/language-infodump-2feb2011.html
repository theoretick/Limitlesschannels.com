Language Infodump 2feb2011

02 Feb 2011 | language | author: theoretick

Looks like a sad day for language learners across the web, Smart.fm returns to its iKnow roots but this time throws up a big ol' paywall.

Some new info over on ionpsych: Effortless Language Learning: not just for kids.

Don't get tricked with the SEO-worthy catchy title,  but some nice reading over on Lousy Linguist about measuring Voice Onset Time in pop culture's "Big Brother" to derive a caste system.

While I personally think Quora is completely useless, maybe Babylon's attempt at making themselves and their 72 million members the "Quora of Linguistics" should pan out into sheer online Web 3.0 awesomeness.

Finally, Radiolab had a great episode a while back on what makes cities cities.  The crazy work they do over at the world-reknown Sante Fe Institute in emergent behavior and complexity gets some application in deriving city size and average number of libraries from walking speed and rate of speech.
