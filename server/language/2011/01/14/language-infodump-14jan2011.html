Language Infodump 14jan2011

14 Jan 2011 | language | author: theoretick

Nice YouTube link on the Spike Jonze-directed music video for The Pharcyde's "Drop" and their linguist's work on teaching the rap backwards.

Yet another prescriptive language Nazi rant over on Slate about hatred for the double-space trend between sentences.  Personally, I've always been a fan.

Keith over on his Extreme Language Learning blog has just completed his 2000 hours of silence project, watching 2000 hours of Chinese television with zero supplementary grammar or language books.  Should be interesting to see how he finally does when he gets around to testing his capability with a speaker.

The Noun Project is an interesting new language project to create a universal (western-centric, clearly) iconography.

Earlier this week the GOP had introduced a new bill to make English the official language of Minnesota.
