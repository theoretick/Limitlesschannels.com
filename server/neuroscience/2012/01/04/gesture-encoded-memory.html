Gesture-Encoded Memory

04 Jan 2012 | neuroscience | author: theoretick

Been reading about gesture-encoded memory a lot lately.  After noticing a recent 2009 paper by Kelly, McDevitt, & Esch on the subject (HERE) I did a bit of research and rediscovered one of the more innovative applications of this through what is known as TPRS, that's Teaching Proficiency through Reading and Storytelling.  Having known someone who went through one of the programs briefly, I began to discuss it with them and they considered it quite effective.  I'll hopefully post a more detailed description in the next few days when I have time.
