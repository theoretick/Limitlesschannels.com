Coldplay fans least likely to have sex on first date, Nirvana fans most likely

16 Aug 2011 | statistics | author: theoretick

Some people over on Tastebuds.fm compiled some data on correlations between favorite bands and likeliness of having sex on first date.  Using data from Last.fm's top 20 artists, Coldplay fans least likely, Nirvana fans most likely.
