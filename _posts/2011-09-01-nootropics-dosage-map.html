Original Methodology

60-day trial of various nootropics in researched "stack" testing cognitive benefits of listed supplements. Each day measures taken in 4 categories on a scale of 1 to 5; 5 being greater. Data-driven cognitive measurement for testing was N-Back performance task using Nguyen's Android App (reviewed HERE)
