last updated March 1st, 2012

My collected sleep data over the period of December 2011 to March 2012. Apparently I had a really amazing and consistent schedule back then... good times.

Much is problematic about using a motion tracker to judge sleep cycles and while the data cannot be a overly accurate depiction of my sleep, it still falls in the "acceptable reliability" range, the theory is that one moves less in deep sleep, due to sleep paralysis.

Data collected with Sleep as Android Android app. If you like playing with data, here's a CSV of the last export. Parsing SaA's data is a total bitch so I also built a converter to make it more readable, you can find that over on Github.
